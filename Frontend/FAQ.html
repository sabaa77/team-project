Frequently Asked Questions

What happens to my order if it cannot be delivered and is returned to Essence Wear?

If you're unavailable to receive your order, our courier will attempt re-delivery. If unsuccessful after multiple tries, the package will be returned to our warehouse and considered undeliverable. We will notify you via email to arrange a refund or reshipment.

How do I track my order?

Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number and a link to track your order.

How long will it take to recieve my order?

The estimated delivery time for your order is calculated by adding the estimated order processing time to the estimated shipping time of your chosen shipping method. The processing period varies for each order and depends on the available status of the items you have selected. For more accurate updates, we recommend tracking your order through the confirmation email sent to you.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, as well as PayPal.
